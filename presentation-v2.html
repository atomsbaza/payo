🤔 The Problem

Receiving crypto payments today is way too complicated

😩 Too Many Steps (6+)

Open wallet → copy address → send via chat → recipient opens wallet → paste → enter amount → confirm

⚠️ Error-Prone

Addresses are 42 characters long. A single wrong character means funds are lost forever with no way to recover.

🔒 Wallet Lock-In

Sender and receiver often need the same wallet app. Different apps mean extra friction switching back and forth.

📱 Unfamiliar UX

People are used to QR-scan-and-pay flows like PromptPay, but crypto still lacks this kind of simplicity.

💡 The Solution

PromptPay-style payment links for crypto
